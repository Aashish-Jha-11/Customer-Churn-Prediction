# Contract Upgrades

## Monthly to Annual Migration
Customers on monthly plans are the most fragile. Offer a meaningful annual discount, typically 15 to 20 percent, to convert them. Bundled free months work better than plain discounts because they feel like a gift.

Source: Telecom Retention Handbook, 2024.

## Multi-Year Discounts
For customers who have already stayed more than 12 months, a two-year contract with 25 percent savings locks in loyal revenue. Pair it with a free service upgrade to reinforce value.

Source: Bain Customer Loyalty Playbook.

## Bundling
Bundling adjacent services, for example data plus streaming or broadband plus phone, raises switching cost. A bundled customer is typically 30 to 50 percent less likely to churn.

Source: Deloitte Telecom Consumer Survey 2023.

## Loyalty Pricing
Honor price protection for long-tenured customers. Raising prices on loyal users is a top three reason for voluntary churn in SaaS and telecom.

Source: ProfitWell Pricing Study 2023.
