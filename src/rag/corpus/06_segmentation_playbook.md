# Segmentation Playbook

## High Value At Risk
Customers in the top spending quartile who also show rising churn probability deserve the fastest human response. A short call from a retention specialist usually recovers these accounts.

Source: McKinsey B2B Retention Study 2022.

## Low Value At Risk
Low-spend customers at risk are often better served by low-touch automated campaigns, such as email nudges or in-app offers. Over-investing in this cohort erodes margin.

Source: ProfitWell Retention Benchmarks 2023.

## Silent Churners
Some customers stop using the product without complaining. Track product usage and flag a 50 percent drop in weekly activity as a silent churn signal. Reach out before the next renewal date.

Source: Amplitude Product Analytics Report 2023.

## Cohort Triage
Group at-risk customers by their dominant risk factor, for example payment delay cohort versus support-heavy cohort, and assign a specific intervention to each cohort. Generic campaigns underperform compared to cohort-specific ones.

Source: Gartner CX Trends Report 2023.
