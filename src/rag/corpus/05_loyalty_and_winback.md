# Loyalty and Winback

## Tiered Rewards
Introduce silver, gold, and platinum tiers based on tenure and spend. Tier progression creates a sunk-cost effect that makes customers hesitant to start over elsewhere.

Source: HBR Reichheld 2014.

## Referral Programs
Customers who refer a friend have roughly double the retention rate of non-referrers because they are emotionally invested in the brand. Offer both parties a credit after the first billing cycle.

Source: Nielsen Global Trust in Advertising.

## NPS-Triggered Perks
When a customer scores the business a 9 or 10 on NPS, send a small thank-you perk within 48 hours. These customers are promoters and should be rewarded before they cool off.

Source: Bain NPS Playbook.

## Winback Offers
For customers who recently cancelled, a targeted offer within the first 30 days wins back about 10 to 15 percent. After 90 days, winback rates drop sharply.

Source: Recurly Churn Report 2023.
