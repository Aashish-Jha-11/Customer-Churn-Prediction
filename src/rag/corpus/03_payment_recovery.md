# Payment Recovery

## Flexible Payment Plans
Offer split-payment or short-term deferral when a customer misses two consecutive invoices. Keeping the relationship alive is cheaper than reacquiring the customer later.

Source: McKinsey Payments Research, 2022.

## Autopay Incentives
A small discount for enabling autopay reduces payment delay and involuntary churn. Involuntary churn from failed cards is often 20 to 40 percent of total churn in subscription businesses.

Source: ProfitWell Retention Benchmarks 2023.

## Dunning Sequence
Send structured payment reminders at day 3, day 7, and day 14 after a missed payment. Use empathetic tone, not threatening. The goal is re-engagement, not collection.

Source: Recurly Subscription Report 2023.

## Grace Periods
A 7-day grace period with no service disruption signals goodwill and keeps trust intact. Immediate service cuts often drive customers to competitors rather than to a payment.

Source: Zuora Subscription Economy Index.
