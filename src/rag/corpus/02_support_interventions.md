# Support Interventions

## Proactive Outreach
When a customer logs three or more support tickets in a month, a proactive call from a senior agent cuts churn by around 20 percent. The conversation should focus on root cause, not ticket closure.

Source: Zendesk CX Benchmark 2023.

## Root Cause Callback
For high-severity tickets, schedule a 48-hour follow-up to confirm the fix held. Customers remember whether the problem came back more than whether it was solved the first time.

Source: Gartner CX Trends Report 2023.

## CSAT-Trigger Playbook
If a post-interaction survey returns a score below 3 out of 5, automatically assign a retention specialist. A human touch within 24 hours recovers roughly half of at-risk accounts.

Source: Forrester Customer Experience Index, 2022.

## Escalation Path
Publish a clear escalation path so frustrated customers do not churn silently. Giving users a way to escalate within the product itself reduces public complaints and negative reviews.

Source: Bain & Company Net Promoter Benchmarks.
